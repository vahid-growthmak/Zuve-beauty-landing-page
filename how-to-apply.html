Home/How to Apply

Ten minutes. One ritual.

Four steps to a 14-day set. No drying time. No appointment. No damage.

01

Prep

Wash hands, push back cuticles, lightly buff the surface of each natural nail. Wipe with the included alcohol pad until completely clean and oil-free.

02

Size & lay out

Hold each ZUVE nail against your finger to find the perfect fit. Lay them in order on a flat surface so you can grab them quickly during application.

03

Peel & press

Peel the adhesive tab from each nail, align with your cuticle, and press firmly for 15–20 seconds. Work pinky to thumb for the cleanest result.

04

Settle

Avoid water for one hour to let the bond cure. That’s it — you’re ready for 7 to 14 days of wear, and the set is reusable up to five times.
