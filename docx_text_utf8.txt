01. PDP PURPOSE &amp; CONVERSION GOALS
Why the PDP is the Most Critical Page
The Product Detail Page is where ZUVE converts browsers into buyers. Unlike the homepage — which communicates dual brand identity — the PDP must do three simultaneous jobs: (1) make the product feel irresistible and premium, (2) remove every possible objection before the customer thinks of it, and (3) make the &apos;Reserve Your Set&apos; action feel inevitable, not urgent.
ZUVE&apos;s PDP must perform at the standard of Charlotte Tilbury&apos;s product pages (cinematic, emotional, trust-rich) while maintaining the restraint of The Ordinary (formula-forward, ingredient-honest) and the scarcity mechanics of Rhode Beauty (drops, waitlists, exclusivity).
