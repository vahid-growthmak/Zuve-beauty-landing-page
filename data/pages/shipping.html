India · All States & UTs

1. Where we ship

We ship across India — metros, tier-2 and tier-3 cities, and rural pin codes wherever our courier partners deliver. For international shipping, write to us at support@zuvebeauty.com for a custom quote.

2. Delivery timelines

  • Metros (Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune): 4–5 working days.
  • Other tier-1 & tier-2 cities: 5–7 working days.
  • Tier-3 and remote locations: 7–10 working days.
  • Order cut-off: orders placed before 14:00 IST ship the same day, Mon–Sat.

3. Shipping charges

  • Free shipping on prepaid orders above ₹1,500.
  • Below ₹1,500: a flat ₹79 shipping fee.
  • Cash on Delivery: available across India with a ₹100 handling fee, regardless of order value.

4. Tracking your order

Once your order ships, you’ll receive an email and WhatsApp message with a tracking link. You can also track at any time on our Track Your Order page using your order number and email.

5. Order changes & cancellations

We process orders quickly. To change or cancel, contact us within 4 hours of placing the order. After that, the order is in transit and follows our Refund Policy on return.

6. Failed or undelivered orders

If a delivery is attempted and refused, or if the address is incorrect or unreachable, the package returns to us. We’ll contact you to either re-ship (re-shipping fee applies) or refund the order minus the original shipping cost.

7. Damaged or missing items

Inspect your parcel on delivery. If anything is damaged or missing, email us within 48 hours at support@zuvebeauty.com with a photo of the parcel and order number, and we’ll replace or refund — no question.
