The Basics

What exactly are ZUVE press-on nails?
ZUVE nails are premium soft-gel press-on sets, hand-finished and salon-grade. They look and feel like extensions but apply in ten minutes, last up to 14 days, and are reusable up to five times. No drying, no UV lamp, no damage.
How long do they last?
With proper application, 7–14 days of everyday wear. For shorter occasions (weddings, photo shoots, a single weekend), use the included tabs for damage-free removal and you can reuse the same set up to five times.
Will they damage my natural nails?
No. ZUVE uses a non-acidic, jelly-glue formula designed to bond cleanly and release without lifting your natural nail. Always follow our removal guide — never rip or force them off.
Are they safe for sensitive nails?
Yes. Our formulations are 10-free, cruelty-free, and dermatologically tested. If you have a known adhesive allergy, patch-test the glue tab on one nail for 24 hours before applying a full set.

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my size?
Each ZUVE set includes 24 nails across 12 sizes, so most people find a perfect match per finger. For a precise read, use our Nail Size Guide — measure each nail at its widest point and match to the chart.
What if my nail size falls between two options?
Always size down. A slightly smaller press-on with a filed edge will hold better than an oversized one. You can lightly file the sides of any ZUVE nail without compromising its strength.
Are ZUVE nails designed for Indian nail beds?
Yes — that’s the heart of why we exist. Most international press-ons run wide or long for the average Indian hand. ZUVE sizing is calibrated specifically for Indian nail proportions.

Application & Wear

How long does application take?
About 10 minutes for a first-timer; under 5 with practice. Full instructions are in our How to Apply guide.
Can I shower, swim, or cook in them?
Yes. ZUVE nails are fully waterproof. We recommend wearing gloves for prolonged water exposure (deep cleaning, long swims) to maximize wear time.
Can I type, work out, or hold a baby?
All of the above, comfortably. Our soft-gel construction has flex, so they move with your hand instead of feeling rigid.
What if one nail pops off?
Every set ships with a spare adhesive tab kit. Lift, clean, re-press — back in business within 30 seconds.

Removal & Reuse

How do I remove them?
Soak your fingertips in warm soapy water for 5 minutes, then gently lift each nail from the cuticle edge using the included wooden stick. Never force.
Can I really reuse the same set?
Yes — up to five wears with care. Soak off any residual adhesive, buff the inside lightly, store in the original case. Many of our community members get 6–8 wears from a single set.
