How to measure

  1. Hold a thin strip of paper across the widest part of each nail.
  2. Mark both edges with a pen.
  3. Measure the distance in millimetres with a ruler.
  4. Match each finger to a size below. Always size down if you’re between two.

A note on fit

A well-fitted press-on should sit flush against your cuticle, cover the full width of your nail, and not pinch the sidewalls. If a nail feels tight, file the inner sides lightly — it won’t compromise strength.
