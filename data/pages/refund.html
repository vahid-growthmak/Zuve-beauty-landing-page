Effective · May 2026

1. The hygiene rule

Because nail products touch skin and nails directly, only unopened, unused sets are eligible for return. Once the seal on the inner case is broken, the product cannot be resold and is not returnable. We’re sorry for the strictness here — it’s about safety, not policy.

2. Eligible returns

  • Unopened, unused sets returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • Manufacturing defects — missing items, wrong shade shipped, broken nails — reported within 48 hours of delivery with photos.
  • Wrong item shipped — we cover both directions of return shipping.

3. How to initiate a return

  1. Email support@zuvebeauty.com with your order number and reason.
  2. For defects, include 2–3 clear photos.
  3. We’ll respond within 1 working day with a return authorisation and pickup details.
  4. Pack the set in its original packaging and hand it to our courier on pickup day.

4. Refund processing

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 working days of us receiving and verifying the return.
  • For COD orders, refunds go to a bank account or UPI ID you provide.
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

5. Exchanges

For wrong-size purchases, we offer one free size exchange per order — subject to stock. Reach out within 7 days of delivery to arrange.

6. Non-returnable items

  • Opened or used nail sets (hygiene).
  • Items marked “Final Sale” or part of a clearance drop.
  • Gift cards.

7. Late or missing refunds

If your refund hasn’t appeared after 7 working days, first check with your bank or UPI provider (processing on their side can add 2–3 days). If it still hasn’t cleared, write to us and we’ll trace it.
