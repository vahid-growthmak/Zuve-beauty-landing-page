2. What we collect

We only collect what we need to ship your order and improve your experience:

  • Identity & contact: name, email, phone, billing & shipping address.
  • Order data: products purchased, payment method (we never store full card numbers), order history.
  • Technical: IP address, device type, browser, pages viewed, anonymised analytics.
  • Communication: messages you send via email, WhatsApp, or our contact form.

3. How we use it

  • To process and ship orders, and handle returns or refunds.
  • To respond to your messages and provide customer support.
  • To send order updates, shipping notifications, and (with your consent) marketing emails.
  • To improve our product, prevent fraud, and meet legal obligations.

4. Who we share it with

We share data only with the partners we need to operate — payment processors, shipping couriers, email and analytics services. They are bound by contract to use your data only for ZUVE’s purposes. We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

5. Cookies

We use functional cookies (cart, login) and analytics cookies (Google Analytics or similar). You can refuse non-essential cookies in your browser settings; the site will still work, but some features (saved cart, login) may be limited.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete it.
  • Withdraw marketing consent at any time (one click in any email).
  • Object to processing or request data portability.

Email support@zuvebeauty.com and we’ll respond within 30 days.

7. Security & retention

We use TLS encryption in transit and PCI-compliant processors for payments. We retain order data for as long as required for tax and legal purposes (typically 7 years in India), and delete marketing data on request.

8. Children

ZUVE products are for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, contact us and we’ll delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we’ll update this page and notify registered customers by email. Continued use of the site after an update means you accept the revised policy.
